# Ramanuja

**Lifespan**: 1017 CE - 1137 CE

**Nationality**: Indian

## Ideas (1)

### Vishishtadvaita (Qualified Non-Dualism)

**Year**: 11th century CE

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

Brahman is qualified by the world and individual souls as its body; reality is non-dual but with internal differentiations

**Tags**: #Vedanta, #Qualified Non-Dualism, #Devotion

**Influenced by**:
- responds to: Adi Shankara's Advaita
- influences: Devotional Hinduism and Vedantic philosophy

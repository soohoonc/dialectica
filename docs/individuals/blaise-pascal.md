**Lifespan**: 1623 CE - 1662 CE

## Ideas (1)

### Pascal's Wager

**Year**: 1670

**Period**: [[periods/modern-philosophy|Modern Philosophy]]

It is rational to believe in God because the potential infinite gain outweighs any finite loss - a probabilistic argument for faith

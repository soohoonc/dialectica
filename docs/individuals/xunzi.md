# Xunzi

**Lifespan**: 313 BCE - 238 BCE

**Nationality**: Chinese

## Ideas (1)

### Human Nature is Evil

**Year**: 3rd century BCE

**Period**: [[periods/ancient-philosophy|Ancient Philosophy]]

Human nature is fundamentally selfish and must be transformed through ritual, education, and social institutions to achieve goodness

**Tags**: #Confucianism, #Human Nature, #Social Institution

**Influenced by**:
- develops: Confucian philosophy in opposition to Mencius
- influences: Legalist political philosophy

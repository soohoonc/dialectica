# Jeremy Bentham

**Lifespan**: 1748 CE - 1832 CE

**Nationality**: English

## Ideas (1)

### Classical Utilitarianism

**Year**: 1789

**Period**: [[periods/modern-philosophy|Modern Philosophy]]

Actions are right insofar as they promote happiness and wrong as they produce the opposite - the greatest happiness for the greatest number

**Tags**: #Utilitarianism, #Ethics, #Legal Philosophy

- develops: [[ideas/utilitarianism|Utilitarianism]]

# Zhu Xi

**Lifespan**: 1130 CE - 1200 CE

**Nationality**: Chinese

## Ideas (1)

### Neo-Confucian Metaphysics of Li and Qi

**Year**: 12th century CE

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

Reality consists of principle (li) and material force (qi); li is the rational structure that qi embodies in particular forms

**Tags**: #Neo-Confucianism, #Metaphysics, #Li and Qi

**Influenced by**:
- synthesizes: Confucian, Buddhist, and Daoist elements
- influences: East Asian philosophical orthodoxy

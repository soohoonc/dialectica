**Lifespan**: 1838 CE - 1900 CE

### The Methods of Ethics

**Year**: 1874

**Period**: [[periods/modern-philosophy|Modern Philosophy]]

Systematically analyzed different ethical methods, showing tensions between egoistic hedonism, universal hedonism, and intuitionism

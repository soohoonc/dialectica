# Friedrich Schelling

**Lifespan**: 1775 CE - 1854 CE

**Nationality**: German

## Ideas (1)

### Philosophy of Nature (Naturphilosophie)

**Year**: 1797

**Period**: [[periods/modern-philosophy|Modern Philosophy]]

Nature and spirit are identical at the absolute level - nature is unconscious spirit, spirit is conscious nature

**Tags**: #German Idealism, #Philosophy of Nature, #Absolute Identity

**Influenced by**:
- builds on: Johann Fichte's absolute idealism
- influences: [[individuals/georg-wilhelm-friedrich-hegel|Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel]]'s system

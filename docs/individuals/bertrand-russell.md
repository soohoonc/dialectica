# Bertrand Russell

**Lifespan**: 1872 CE - 1970 CE

**Nationality**: British

## Ideas (1)

### Logical Atomism

**Year**: 1918

**Period**: [[periods/contemporary-philosophy|Contemporary Philosophy]]

The world consists of logical atoms (facts) that can be analyzed through formal logic and correspond to atomic propositions

**Tags**: #Analytic Philosophy, #Logic, #Philosophy of Language

**Influenced by**:
- collaborates with: Alfred North Whitehead on Principia Mathematica
- influences: [[individuals/ludwig-wittgenstein|Ludwig Wittgenstein]]'s early philosophy

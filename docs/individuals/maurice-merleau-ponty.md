**Lifespan**: 1908 CE - 1961 CE

## Ideas (1)

### Embodied Perception

**Year**: 1945

**Period**: [[periods/contemporary-philosophy|Contemporary Philosophy]]

Perception is primarily bodily and pre-reflective - we perceive the world through our lived, embodied experience rather than abstract thought

**Tags**: #Phenomenology, #Embodiment, #Perception

**Influenced by**:
- builds on: Edmund Husserl's phenomenology

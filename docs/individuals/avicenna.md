# Avicenna (Ibn Sina)

**Lifespan**: 980 CE - 1037 CE

**Nationality**: Persian

## Ideas (1)

### Distinction of Essence and Existence

**Year**: 11th century CE

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

In all things except God, essence and existence are distinct; God is the only necessary being whose essence is existence

**Tags**: #Islamic Philosophy, #Metaphysics, #Essence and Existence

**Influenced by**:
- develops: Aristotelian metaphysics
- influences: [[individuals/thomas-aquinas|Thomas Aquinas]] and scholastic philosophy

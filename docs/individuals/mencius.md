# Mencius

**Lifespan**: 372 BCE - 289 BCE

**Nationality**: Chinese

## Ideas (1)

### Innate Goodness of Human Nature

**Year**: 4th century BCE

**Period**: [[periods/ancient-philosophy|Ancient Philosophy]]

Human nature is originally good; all people possess innate moral sentiments that can be cultivated through proper education

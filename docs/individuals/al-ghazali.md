# Al-Ghazali

**Lifespan**: 1058 CE - 1111 CE

**Nationality**: Persian

## Ideas (1)

### Critique of Philosophy (Tahāfut al-Falāsifa)

**Year**: 1095

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

Philosophical reason has limits; religious knowledge comes through direct mystical experience rather than rational demonstration

**Tags**: #Islamic Philosophy, #Mysticism, #Critique of Reason

**Influenced by**:
- critiques: Avicenna and Islamic Aristotelians
- influences: Islamic theology and mysticism

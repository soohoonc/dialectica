# Nagarjuna

**Lifespan**: 150 CE - 250 CE

**Nationality**: Indian

## Ideas (1)

### Madhyamaka and Emptiness (Śūnyatā)

**Year**: 2nd century CE

**Period**: [[periods/ancient-philosophy|Ancient Philosophy]]

All phenomena lack inherent existence (svabhāva) and arise through dependent origination - emptiness is the ultimate nature of reality

**Tags**: #Buddhism, #Emptiness, #Middle Way

**Influenced by**:
- develops: Mahāyāna Buddhist philosophy
- influences: Tibetan and East Asian Buddhist thought

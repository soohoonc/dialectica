# Madhvacharya

**Lifespan**: 1238 CE - 1317 CE

**Nationality**: Indian

## Ideas (1)

### Dvaita Vedanta (Dualism)

**Year**: 13th century CE

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

God (Vishnu), individual souls, and the world are eternally distinct realities - ultimate dualism rather than non-dualism

**Tags**: #Vedanta, #Dualism, #Hindu Philosophy

**Influenced by**:
- opposes: Advaitic non-dualism
- influences: Dualistic Hindu philosophical schools

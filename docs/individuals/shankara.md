### Advaita Vedanta (Non-Dualism)

**Year**: 8th century CE

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

Ultimate reality is Brahman - pure, undifferentiated consciousness; individual souls and the world are illusory appearances

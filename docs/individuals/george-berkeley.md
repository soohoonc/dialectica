# George Berkeley

**Lifespan**: 1685 - 1753

**Nationality**: Irish

## Ideas (1)

### [[ideas/immaterialism|Immaterialism]]

**Year**: 1710

**Period**: [[periods/early-modern-philosophy|Early Modern Philosophy]]

The thesis that material objects do not exist independently of perception; "esse est percipi" (to be is to be perceived)

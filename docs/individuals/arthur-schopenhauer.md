# Arthur Schopenhauer

**Lifespan**: 1788 CE - 1860 CE

**Nationality**: German

## Ideas (1)

### The World as Will and Representation

**Year**: 1818

**Period**: [[periods/modern-philosophy|Modern Philosophy]]

The world as we experience it is representation, but its underlying reality is an irrational, blind Will that drives all existence and suffering

**Tags**: #Pessimism, #Will, #Eastern Philosophy

**Influenced by**:
- builds on: [[individuals/immanuel-kant|Immanuel Kant]]'s distinction between phenomena and noumena
- influences: [[individuals/friedrich-nietzsche|Friedrich Nietzsche]]'s [[ideas/will-to-power|Will to Power]]

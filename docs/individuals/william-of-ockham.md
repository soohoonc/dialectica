### [[ideas/ockhams-razor|Ockham's Razor]]

**Year**: 1320

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

The principle of parsimony stating that entities should not be multiplied beyond necessity; the simplest explanation is usually the correct one

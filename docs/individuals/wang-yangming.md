# Wang Yangming

**Lifespan**: 1472 CE - 1529 CE

**Nationality**: Chinese

## Ideas (1)

### Unity of Knowledge and Action

**Year**: 16th century CE

**Period**: [[periods/modern-philosophy|Modern Philosophy]]

Mind and principle are one; genuine knowledge is inseparable from moral action - to know good is to do good

**Tags**: #Neo-Confucianism, #Mind Philosophy, #Ethics

**Influenced by**:
- opposes: Zhu Xi's metaphysical dualism
- influences: East Asian idealist philosophy

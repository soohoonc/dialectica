# Mozi

**Lifespan**: 470 BCE - 391 BCE

**Nationality**: Chinese

## Ideas (1)

### Universal Love (Jian Ai)

**Year**: 5th century BCE

**Period**: [[periods/ancient-philosophy|Ancient Philosophy]]

Moral action requires universal, impartial love for all people rather than preferential care based on family or social relationships

**Tags**: #Mohism, #Universal Love, #Consequentialism

**Influenced by**:
- opposes: Confucian particularism
- parallels: [[ideas/utilitarianism|Utilitarian]] thinking

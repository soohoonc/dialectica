# Dōgen Zenji

**Lifespan**: 1200 CE - 1253 CE

**Nationality**: Japanese

## Ideas (1)

### Shikantaza (Just Sitting) and Buddha-Nature

**Year**: 13th century CE

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

Sitting meditation (zazen) is not a means to enlightenment but the expression of inherent Buddha-nature - practice and realization are one

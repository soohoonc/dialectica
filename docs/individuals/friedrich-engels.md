### Dialectical Materialism

**Year**: 1878

**Period**: [[periods/modern-philosophy|Modern Philosophy]]

The natural and social world develops through dialectical processes of contradiction and resolution based on material conditions

**Tags**: #Dialectical Materialism, #Political Philosophy, #Science

- collaborates with: Karl Marx on historical materialism
- applies: [[ideas/hegelian-dialectic|Hegelian Dialectic]] to material reality

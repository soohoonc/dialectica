### Cyclical Theory of Civilizations

**Year**: 1377

**Period**: [[periods/medieval-philosophy|Medieval Philosophy]]

Civilizations rise and fall in predictable cycles driven by group solidarity (asabiyyah) and social, economic, and political forces

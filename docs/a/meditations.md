# Meditations

_Meditations_ (Greek: Τὰ εἰς ἑαυτόν) is a series of personal writings by Marcus Aurelius, Roman Emperor from 161 to 180 CE.

## Content

Written as a source of personal guidance and self-improvement, the Meditations present Stoic philosophy as a practical guide to life.

## Key Themes

- Acceptance of fate and natural order
- Focus on what is within our control
- The transience of all things
- Duty and service to others
- Inner peace through rational thought

# The Starry Night

_The Starry Night_ (Dutch: _De sterrennacht_) is an oil-on-canvas painting by Vincent van Gogh, created in June 1889.

## Description

The painting depicts a swirling night sky over a village, with a prominent cypress tree in the foreground. The moon and stars are surrounded by dramatic halos of light.

## Context

Van Gogh painted this while staying at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum in Saint-Rémy-de-Provence, France, following his mental breakdown.

## Artistic Features

- Bold, expressive brushwork
- Vivid blues and yellows
- Swirling, dynamic sky
- Contrast between the turbulent sky and peaceful village

## Location

Housed in the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York City since 1941.

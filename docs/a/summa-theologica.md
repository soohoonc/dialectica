# Summa Theologica

The *Summa Theologica* (or *Summa Theologiae*) is the best-known work of Thomas Aquinas (1225â€“1274).

## Content

An encyclopedic synthesis of Christian theology and Aristotelian philosophy, the Summa addresses questions about God, morality, and Christ.

## Structure

The work is organized into questions, each divided into articles following a standard format:
1. Objections to the position
2. "On the contrary" (sed contra)
3. Aquinas's answer (respondeo)
4. Replies to objections

## Key Topics

- The Five Ways (proofs of God's existence)
- Natural law theory
- The virtues
- The nature of God

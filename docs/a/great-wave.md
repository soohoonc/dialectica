# The Great Wave off Kanagawa

*The Great Wave off Kanagawa* (Japanese: 神奈川沖浪裏) is a woodblock print by the Japanese ukiyo-e artist Hokusai, published between 1829 and 1833.

## Description

The print depicts an enormous wave threatening boats off the coast of Kanagawa Prefecture, with Mount Fuji visible in the background.

## Artistic Features

- Dramatic composition with the wave dominating the scene
- Prussian blue imported from Europe
- Mount Fuji appears small but central
- Dynamic tension between nature and human vessels

## Series

Part of the series *Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji* (富嶽三十六景), exploring the sacred mountain from various locations and seasons.

## Influence

One of the most recognized works of Japanese art, it influenced Western Impressionists and remains an iconic image worldwide.

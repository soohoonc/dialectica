# Phenomenology of Spirit

The *Phenomenology of Spirit* (German: *Ph√§nomenologie des Geistes*) is G.W.F. Hegel's most important work, published in 1807.

## Content

Hegel traces the development of consciousness from immediate sense-certainty to absolute knowledge, showing how Spirit comes to know itself.

## Key Concepts

- **Dialectic**: Thesis, antithesis, synthesis
- **Master-Slave Dialectic**: Self-consciousness emerges through recognition
- **Unhappy Consciousness**: The divided self seeking unity
- **Absolute Spirit**: The culmination of historical development

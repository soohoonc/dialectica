# Confessions

The *Confessions* (Latin: *Confessiones*) is an autobiographical work by Augustine of Hippo, written between 397 and 400 CE.

## Content

Part autobiography, part theological meditation, the Confessions traces Augustine's spiritual journey from sinful youth to Christian conversion.

## Key Themes

- The nature of memory and time
- Original sin and grace
- The restless heart seeking God
- The problem of evil
- Neoplatonic influence on Christian thought

## Famous Lines

"You have made us for yourself, O Lord, and our heart is restless until it rests in you."

# Thomism

Thomism is the scholastic philosophical and theological tradition associated with [[thomas-aquinas]]. In this graph it connects Aristotelian metaphysics, moral reasoning, and medieval institutional thought.

## Core Claim

The tradition develops themes from [[forms]] and [[natural-law]], while opposing positions grounded in value negation such as [[nihilism]]. It is frequently read alongside [[deontology]] and [[virtue-ethics]].

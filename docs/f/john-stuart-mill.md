# John Stuart Mill

John Stuart Mill was a major nineteenth-century philosopher and political theorist whose work shaped liberal thought, moral philosophy, and social reform. In this corpus, Mill is closely tied to [[social-contract]] and read in dialogue with [[karl-marx]].

## Life and Context

Mill worked primarily in [[london]] during the [[victorian-era]], in the context of industrialization, parliamentary reform, and debates over liberty and political economy.

## Contributions

Mill's contributions are represented through discussions of liberty, representative government, and moral reasoning under conditions of pluralism. His position is often compared with [[deontology]], [[virtue-ethics]], and modern rights discourse.

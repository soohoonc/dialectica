# Confucius

Confucius was an influential thinker whose work shaped major debates in philosophy, science, and political thought. In Dialectica, this figure is connected to [[confucian-ethics]] and placed in relation to [[aristotle]].

## Life and Context

Confucius worked across institutions and cities that defined the intellectual climate of the period. Key locations include qufu, luoyang, and the primary historical frame is [[late-zhou]].

## Contributions

The most important contributions associated with Confucius can be read through [[confucian-ethics]]. Their work also sits in productive tension with ideas such as [[deontology]], [[virtue-ethics]], and [[forms]].
